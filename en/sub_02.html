What is Bond?

an investment product that seeks stability and profitability by paying back the principal and a fixed return

It is a type of borrowing certificate issued by the government, public institutions, financial institutions, and corporations with high credit ratings for fundraising.

  • profitability

Flexible Bond Asset Allocation

We dynamically adjust bond asset allocation based on market conditions to manage threats and capture revenue opportunities. This approach actively adjusts the ratio of asset classes according to market volatility, minimizing risk and maximizing potential returns.

Theme-Based Investing

We explore high growth potential through investments focused on long-term global trends and innovative industries. This strategy leverages megatrends such as technological advancements, demographic shifts, and environmental sustainability.

Harmony of Value and Growth Investing

By simultaneously investing in undervalued value bonds and growth assets with high growth potential in the market, we pursue diversity and balance in the portfolio.

ESG and Sustainable Investing

We emphasize long-term sustainability and social responsibility by investing in companies that meet Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) criteria. This approach integrates social value and environmental responsibility into investment decisions.
